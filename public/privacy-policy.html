Privacy Policy for SubTrack

Last updated: January 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

SubTrack stores all your subscription data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal subscription information on any external servers.

2. Local Storage

All subscription data including names, amounts, and renewal dates are stored locally using your device's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

3. AI Processing

If you use the AI receipt scanning feature with your own Gemini API key, image processing occurs through Google's Gemini API. We do not store or access these images.

4. Notifications

Push notifications are generated locally based on your subscription dates and reminder settings.

5. Contact

For privacy questions: subtrack.support@email.com
